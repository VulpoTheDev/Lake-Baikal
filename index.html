What's Lake Baikal All About?

We're talking about the oldest and deepest lake in the world Located in Siberia Russia! The river being discussed about is named Lake Baikal. Lake Baikal is a lake located in Russia. It's the oldest (25 million years old) and deep as heck (1,700 meters). The Lake is rich with various plants and animals. It's continet is in the Eurasia.

The Enviroment Within Lake Balkai

The Tempature of Lake Balkai is -18°C (on the coolest month) and 27°C (on the warmest month). There are at least six main bays on the lake, around two dozen coves, and many other shallow bays and when it comes to the lake's outlet Angara is the only outlet of the lake and flows westward from the southwestern end
